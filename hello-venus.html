About Me

I’m currently a freshman student attending CIA with hopes of going into an industrial design major. I first got into the idea of pursuing industrial design from watching Dad work as a kid and learning through him what it was all about. I see Industrial design as a combination of drawing and model building with problem solving and collaboration. I’ve also been thinking about the environment and how we can create environmentally friendly designs.
